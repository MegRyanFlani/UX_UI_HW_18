About Me

Hi, I'm Meg Ryan Flanigan, & these are things about me!

The things I do well include art, design, and research (I also tend bar very well, but thats for holiday parties). After freelancing for many years, I began a deep dive into UX/UI, inspired by a long-time interest in how people think and act. Being able to apply a creative approach and great looking aesthetic to those thoughts and actions is something I enjoy doing. The goal is to continue doing those things, while learning how to to do them better.

My other interests include the occasional bartending gig, selling my art at festivals, running, hiking, and exploring places with my friends.

More often than not, I am accompanied by my dog, Quinn.
